--- title: Download --- For a quick start, you can use this snippet in your maven POM: ~~~ #!xml br.com.caelum vraptor <%= Maven.vraptor_version %> ~~~ Or you can download it directly [at our artifacts repository](https://bintray.com/caelum/VRaptor4/br.com.caelum.vraptor/). At the same repository you can download our sample project, the `vraptor-musicjungle`. There is also the `vraptor-blank-project`, a base project configured with all required dependencies.
